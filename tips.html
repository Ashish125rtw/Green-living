Green Living Tips

  • Turn off lights when not in use.
  • Use energy-efficient appliances.
  • Install solar panels if possible.
  • Insulate your home to reduce heating and cooling costs.

  • Recycle paper, plastic, glass, and metal.
  • Compost organic waste.
  • Reduce single-use plastics.
  • Buy products with minimal packaging.

  • Fix leaks promptly.
  • Install water-saving fixtures.
  • Take shorter showers.
  • Collect rainwater for gardening.

  • Use public transportation.
  • Carpool or ride-share.
  • Bike or walk when possible.
  • Drive fuel-efficient vehicles.

  • Eat locally-sourced food.
  • Reduce meat consumption.
  • Choose organic and sustainably grown products.
  • Grow your own vegetables.
